Arsi University Student Caffee location


Arsi University is located in Asella, the capital city of the Arsi Zone in the Oromia Region of Ethiopia. Asella is situated about 175 kilometers southeast of Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia.


The Caffee at Arsi University is typically located within the university campus in Asella. Since universities often have central dining areas for students and staff, the cafeteria is likely situated near the main academic buildings or student activity areas.
