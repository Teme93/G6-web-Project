We Are Committed To Offering Fresh And Friendly Meals!!

Students getting to the cafes for meal.

Arsi University is located in Asella, the capital city of the Arsi Zone in the Oromia Region of Ethiopia. Asella is situated about 175 kilometers southeast of Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia.


The Cafe at Arsi University is typically located within the university campus in Asella. Since universities often have central dining areas for students and staff, the cafeteria is likely situated near the main academic buildings or student activity areas.


About Us

Welcome to Arsi University Students Cafe, your premier destination for delicious and freely offord meals on campus. Established in 15 October 2014, we have been serving the university community with pride, offering a welcoming atmosphere and nutritious meals that fuel academic success.


Located at the heart of the university campus, our cafe is committed to providing quality food options for Students. We use fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create meals that satisfy traditional tastes.
